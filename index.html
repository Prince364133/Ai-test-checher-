📄 AI Answer Sheet Grader 📝

This website is an "AI Answer Sheet Grader" designed to help teachers and students automatically grade answer sheets. It allows users to paste student responses, select a class, subject, and publisher, and receive AI-graded feedback. The tool highlights student mistakes, correct answers, and assigns marks, all while comparing the answers against correct solutions retrieved from the internet.

How to Use:

  1. 📋 Paste the Answer Sheet: Enter or paste the student's responses in the provided text area.
  2. 📚 Class, Subject, and Publisher: Select the class and enter the subject and publisher details.
  3. 💡 Process with ChatGPT: Generate a detailed analysis prompt, copy it, and open ChatGPT to get the AI's response.
  4. 🔍 Highlight and Grade: Paste the processed ChatGPT result back into the site to see highlighted questions, student answers, and correct responses with marks.
  5. 📥 Download the Results: Generate and download the highlighted result as a PDF.
